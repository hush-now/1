Week 9

Week 9 (23 October - 29 October)

Week 9 (23 October - 29 October)


Week 9 Schedule


At 8:45 am Beijing time on October 31, members of the Hushnow team collectively attended a laboratory safety course. The laboratory instructor explained the safety instructions of the laboratory to us. At the same time, teach the use of 3D printer process. First of all, the teacher introduced the use process of careful printer and the slicing method of 3D drawings of parts, including how to feed and print by 3D printer. This part of the teacher stressed some safety instructions in the operation, for example, the temperature of the 3D printer spray gun is about 200 degrees Celsius, and it must be fully preheated before feeding. After that, the graduate student introduced the operation process of the large printer to us, which is similar to the small printer. This course will be of great help to our work after the group project. Our Serpentine robot project is expected to start parts production in March 2024, mainly focusing on 3d printing to produce various parts including the robot body. At the same time, the safety of the laboratory is related to our personal and property safety, which deserves our attention.
