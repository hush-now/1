Week 3 (11 September - 17 September)

Week 3 (11 September - 17 September)


Week 3 Schedule


This week, our group had a productive discussion about the goals, overall plan, and execution steps for our graduation project. We focused on brainstorming and setting clear objectives for our project. Through collaborative efforts, we were able to establish a shared understanding of the project’s purpose and scope. During the discussion, we analyzed various factors that could impact the success of our project, such as time constraints, available resources, and potential challenges. We also identified the key stakeholders and their roles in supporting our project. In terms of the overall plan, we outlined a step-by-step approach to ensure a smooth execution of our project. This included tasks such as conducting research, gathering relevant data, designing prototypes, and testing them. We also allocated responsibilities to each team member based on their skills and interests. Furthermore, we discussed the importance of effective communication and regular progress updates within the group. We agreed to hold weekly meetings to discuss any issues or concerns that may arise during the project implementation. This will help us stay on track and make any necessary adjustments to ensure the project’s success. Overall, our group’s discussion on the goals, overall plan, and execution steps for our graduation project was fruitful and insightful. We have a clear direction and a solid plan in place, which will guide us in achieving our objectives effectively. We are confident that with our collective efforts and commitment, we will be able to accomplish our goals within the given time frame.
